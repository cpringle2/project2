Why Schools Don't Have Fine Arts

Call to Action

Lots of schools don't include Fine Arts in part of their daily curricular simply because of money. Money is a big issue when
it comes to academic classes, especially when the government doesn't require for the classes to be taken to graduate from
high school.

High schools claim that having Fine Arts programs won't bring in as much money as sports. They say that people come to and pay for sports more often then people would for a showcase or play. The only reason why more people may come to sporting events is because it sits more people at a time.
