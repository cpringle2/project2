Importance of Fine Arts

Why Schools Don't Have Fine Arts

Call to Action

The Arts can really help students develop lots of skills. Those skills may include creativity, self-expression,
risk-taking, dedication, perseverance: the list goes on and on.

The skills can be applied outside of the arts as well. Students can use these skills to express their more creative side in lots of other classes,
especially in English.
